About Me

I was born in Sierra Vista, AZ on October 23rd, 1983. As a native to southeast Arizona, the heat doesn't bother me too much, and I have an affinity for burritos, raspados (when I can get my hands on them), Eegee's, and other things that make this part of the country a truly unique to place to live.

My goal with this bootcamp is to do a complete shift in career. Previously, I have worked in management at The Melting Pot, Jimmy John's, and QuikTrip. After close to 12 years working holidays, weekends, and anywhere from 50-70 hour work weeks, I wanted to do something for myself. My hope after six months in bootcamp is to have a fresh start and have in demand skills.

While I am not studying, I enjoy spending time with my family (three kids and my wife), trying different types of beer, listening to music, and gaming (both tabletop and electronic).
